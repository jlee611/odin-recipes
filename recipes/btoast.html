Description

A basic but tasty and nutritious breakfast toast that will keep you full and ready to rock and roll.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of toast of choice
  • 1 cup of cottage Cheese
  • 2 tbsp of jam of choice
  • 2 tspn of hemp seeds

Steps

  1. Slice bread
  2. Toast bread to desired preference
  3. Spread jam of choice
  4. Spread cottage cheese over jam
  5. Sprinkle hemp seeds on top of cottage cheese
  6. Enjoy!
