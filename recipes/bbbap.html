Description

A Korean classic. Delicious, cheap, and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 2 cup of rice of choice
  • 1 tbsp of sesame oil
  • 2 tspn of olive oil
  • 2 tbsp of kochujang sauce
  • 2 cup of meat of choice
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of sliced carrots
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1 avocado
  • Sesame seeds
  • Seaweed flakes
  • Spinach
  • Optional: Momofuku Chili Crunch

Steps

  1. Cook 2 cups of rice.
  2. Add in 2 tspn of olive oil in pan. Medium heat.
  3. Chop and grill 1 cup of white onions.
  4. Grill 2 cups of meat.
  5. Mix 2 cups of spinach and 1 tbsp of olive oil then grill.
  6. Place cooked rice in bowl.
  7. Place grilled onion, spinach, meat, sliced carrots, sliced avocado on top of rice.
  8. Cook egg to desired preference. Place cooked egg on top of everything.
  9. Add 1 tbsp of sesame oil. 2 tbsp of kochujang sauce, 2 tbsp of Momofuku Chili Crunch (optional), sesame seeds, seaweed flakes on top of everything.
  10. Mix thoroughly.
  11. Enjoy!
