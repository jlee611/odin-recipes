Description

A modern classic with some extra attitude.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of preferred bread
  • 1 large avocados
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 large egg
  • (Optional): Momofuku Chili Crunch

Steps

  1. Toast bread to desired preference.
  2. Slice and chop avocado.
  3. Cook and scramble eggs. Chop it once it's done.
  4. Cook bacon. Chop or slice (whichever you prefer).
  5. Layer the avocado, eggs, and bacon on top of the toast.
  6. Optional: Add Momofuku Chili Crunch on top of it all.
  7. Enjoy!
